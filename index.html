About Us

Welcome home to Dusty Woods Apts! Conveniently located off State Route and Main Rd. Dusty Woods Apts is near plenty of shopping, dining, and entertainment options and just a short drive to campus. We also have great internet connectivity throughout the community making it ideal for people working at home or students studying coding.

Dusty Woods Apts features newly-renovated 1, 2, and 3-bedroom apartments and carriage homes with today’s necessities. Our pet-friendly homes are complete with a private patio or balcony, walk-in closets, and eat-in dining areas. Ask about our one-bedroom plus den, it’s perfect for a home office or guest bedroom.
